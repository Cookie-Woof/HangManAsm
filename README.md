you will be given a topic, for example: "food", and a certion amount 
of letters are represented by dashes _ _ _ _ _.

Number one: Player #2 guessed a letter that was part of the word correctly and the 
place of the letter is revaled lets say - Player #2 guessed A and the word is "Apple"
so Player #1 revales the place of the letter: A _ _ _ _ and thats all.

Number two: Player #2 guessed a letter that was *not* part of the word and from then the hanging man starts to apper
for every worng guess another part of its body will be showen the moment the man is complete Player #2 loses the game and
Player #1 wins!
